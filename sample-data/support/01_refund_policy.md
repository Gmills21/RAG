# Refund Policy

**Version**: 2.1  
**Last Updated**: 2025-12-15  
**Owner**: Finance Team  

## Overview

This document outlines the company's refund policy for all subscription and service purchases.

## Standard Refund Policy

### 30-Day Refund Window

Customers may request a full refund within **30 days** of their initial purchase or subscription renewal.

**Eligibility**:
- Request must be made within 30 days of the charge date
- Account must not be flagged for fraud or abuse
- Customer must provide a valid reason for the refund

**Process**:
1. Customer contacts support with refund request
2. Support verifies account status and charge date
3. Support submits refund request to Finance
4. Finance processes refund within 5-7 business days

## Exceptions and Special Cases

### Annual Enterprise Contracts

**Annual enterprise contracts require Finance Director approval for refunds.**

- Contracts over $10,000 require written approval
- Partial refunds may be calculated on a pro-rated basis
- Account termination may be required for full refunds

### High-Value Transactions

Invoices over **$5,000** require Finance approval before processing any refund.

**Approval process**:
1. Support escalates to Finance team
2. Finance Director reviews transaction history
3. Decision communicated within 2 business days

### Prohibited Refunds

**Do NOT process refunds for**:
- Chargebacks (escalate to Finance immediately)
- Suspected fraud or terms-of-service violations
- Accounts with outstanding payments or arrears
- Purchases older than 30 days without Director approval

## Customer Communication

### Approved Refunds

**Customer-ready template**:
```
We've processed your refund request. You'll see the credit on your original payment 
method within 5-7 business days. Your subscription has been canceled as of [date].

If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to reach out.
```

### Denied Refunds

**Escalation required**: If a refund request is denied, escalate to your team lead before responding to the customer.
